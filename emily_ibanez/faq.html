FAQ

Look no further to answer your questions! All commonly asked questions are answered here. If you are wondering about something that is not answered here our email is in our contact page.

What is a content creator?

Content creators in our club's context is anyone who creates media that is able to be viewed or broadcasted digitally such as Livestreams, YouTube videos and Podcasts.

Who can join?

Anyone who is a Sheridan College student and is interested is welcome to join! There is no experience needed to become a member of the Webcast club.

What can I look forward to?

There are a variety of different events and activities schedules for our club such as workshops, live tutorials, hangouts and much more!
